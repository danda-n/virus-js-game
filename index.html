FAQ

What is this?

This is a simple virus simluation system, where you create 100 people and they infect/heal/shoot eachother.

How do you "simulate"?

You hit the button START and population is created, then you can watch and enhance the game to your liking.

What else can you do?

You can create a doctor, that moves around the map and heals infected people as well as couple of buildings, which you can move around the map.

What kind of buildings?

Clinic, which makes infected people immune to further infection. Army base, which shoots infected people when in range and a helicopter, which grabs healthy people around and at specified amount inside, leaves the world =)

What else is there?

You have simple legend, which person-color means what, as well as 2 graphs displaying infection rate and mortality rate.

- and ultimately you can save your progress and continue where you left.
